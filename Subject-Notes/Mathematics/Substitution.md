# Number Laws

## Commutative Law

- $x+y = y+x$
- $x-y \ne y-x$
- $x\times y = y \times x$
- $x\div y \neq y \div x$

## Associative Law

- $x+(y+z) = (x+y)+z$
- $x-(y-z)\ne (x-y)-z$
- $x \times(y\times z)=(x\times y)\times z$
- $x \div(y\div z) \ne (x \div y) \div z$

## Identity Law

- $x+0 = 0+x = x$
- $x - 0 \ne 0 -x$
- $x\times 1 = 1 \times x = x$
- $x \div 1 \ne 1 \div x$

## Inverse Law

- $x +-x = -x +- =0$
- $x\times \frac{1}{x}=\frac{1}{x}\times x = 1$
