# Notation

- $m =$ size of train set, amount of training samples.
- $n =$ amount of input variables, e.g. $x_1, x_2, x_3 ...x_n$
- $L =$ Amount of layers in NN
    - $l =$ specific layer
- $W^l$ = weight(s), $l=$ layer the weights are going in to
